Pizza



Description

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best accompanied by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano/li>
  • Tomato products
  • ½ cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 6 leaves fresh basil, torn

Steps>

Here's a very brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. MMake the dough: Pour warm water into a large bowl, then sprinkle yeast over the top. Let stand for 5 minutes. Stir in cold water and salt. Stir in flour, 1 cup at a time until incorporated.
  2. CMake the pizzas: Remove dough from the refrigerator one hour prior to using.
  3. Preheat the oven, with a pizza stone on the lowest rack, to 550 degrees F (288 degrees C). Lightly dust a pizza peel with flour.
